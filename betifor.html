You must try these soft and crunchy buiscuit that just melts in your mouth and cant get away with only one biscuit and to make them you'll need:

1cup butter
1/2 + 1/4 cup powdered sugar
2 egg yolks
1 tsp vanilla extract
A Pinch of salt
1 tsp baking powder
2 cups flour

Directions:

Preheat your oven at 180 degrees celcius. In a large bowl, mix your butter until it becomes fluffy.
Add your powered sugar, vanilla and the egg yolks and miss them all toether well, until it becomes light and fluffy.

Next that goes in is the baking powder and salt and mix. Gradually add your flour and fold it in with a spatula.
Separate your mixture into 2 and you can flavour it with some cocoa powder. After your done, refrigerate it for 10 minutes.

Now comes the fun part, shaping them. In this part you can use your Creativity and shape your dough.
We used a biscuit maker and made little shapes. You can use cookie cutter, molds or even a fork like what we baked in our Ma'amool recepie .

Once you are all done bake it in the oven for 10 minutes and enjoy it with a cup of coffee.

Hope you enjoied the recepie, share it with your friends and make it together!
