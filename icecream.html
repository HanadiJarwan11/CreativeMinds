We made a simple, delicious vanilla ice cream to enjoy at anytime. Here is how you can make it too:

Milk powder mixture:
1/3 cup milk powder
1/3 cup water

Whipped cream mixture
1 cup heavy whipping cream
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 cup sugar

Direction
To make the Milk powder mixture you'll mix the milk powder and water, in a bowl, really well until there are no lumps.

In a bigger bowl, combine the whipped cream and vanilla extract untl you get soft peaks
Gradually add your sugar while mixing and later your milk powder mixture, mix until everything becomes smooth.

Pour it in a plastic or a glass container, cover it with plastic wrap and put it in the freezer for 2 hours

Remove it from the freezer, give it a mix so that it doest crystalise and put it back in the freezer for another 2 hours.
Repeat this process one more time to get it smooth.The more you stir it the creamer it gets.
freeze it one last time for 8 hours or overnight.

Serve it in a small bowl or an icecream cone and top it off with some of the chocolate sauce that is in the Chocolate Cake Recepie
or turn it into a milkshake and enjoy.

It tastes so good you have to try it!
Share it with your friends and those who love icecream
and make it together!
