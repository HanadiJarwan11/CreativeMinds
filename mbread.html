The recepie

To make the dough you will need:

One cup of milk
3/4 cup of oil
1/4 cup sugar
1 large egg (room temperature)
1 tablespoon yeast
1 teaspoon salt
3 1/4 cup flour

Caramel recipe:
2 tbs of butter
1 cup sugar
1/3 cup milk
Pinch of salt
1 teaspoon cornstarch +1 tablespoon milk

Cinnamon and sugar mixture:
2 tablespoons cinnamon
3 tablespoons powdered sugar

The Direction:

Bake it for 180 degrease for about 15-20 minutes.
