Ingredients:

12 Dates
2 cups of flour
1/2+ 1/8 cups of margarine
In 1/2 cup, add 3 tablespoons of milk and the rest with condensed milk

Direction:

Remove the pits from the dates and mush them with your hands and roll them into little balls

In a large bowl, combine the flour, margarine and the milk + condensed milk and mix it all together well.

Roll the dough into balls and make an ndent in them and place the date ball, cover it, roll it and place it into a mold
that prints out a pretty shape and place n in a parchment paper or aluminium foil.
If you dont have a mold, you can just roll them into balls and press it with a fork to make a criss cross shape,
you can be as creative as you want.

Once you're done arranging them, bake it in the oven at 130 degrees celsius for 10 minutes or until golden and brown.
Sprinke them with powered sugar and enjoy the taste of Ma'amool
