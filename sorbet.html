To make a frozen sorbe you'll need:

1 frozen diced mango (any fruit of your choice)
1-2 squeeses of lime

Direction:
Put the mangos into a blender and blend until smooth
Squeeze in your lime and blend for a few seconds. Pour it into a bowl
and you are ready to enjoy your frozen sorbet during the Summer!
