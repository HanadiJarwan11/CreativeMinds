We made a delicious chocolate cake topped off with some rich chocolate sauce.

Here is the recepie and the directions to make it:
2 eggs (room temp)
3/4 cup of sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 cup of oil
3/4 cup of milk (room temp)

For the dry ingredients: (sifted)
1 1/2 cup of flour
1/2 cup of cocoa powder
1 teaspoon of baking powder
1 teaspoon of baking soda
1 teaspoon of nescaf√©
pinch of salt

For the chocolate sauce:
1/2 cup of cocoa powder
1/2 tin of cream
1/2 tin of condensed milk

Direction:
Grab a large bowl and crack in your room temperatured eggs and add your sugar.
At this point, make sure to preheat your oven at 180 degree celsius.

Mix your wet ingredients until light and fluffy and has lightened in color.
Pour the vanilla extract and mix, oil and milk goes in next and mix again.
Add in all the dry ingredients and mix until combined, dont over mix it in this step!

Pour your hot water and pour the batter in small individual ramekins or a cake pan.
Bake it in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until you insert a toothpick inside the cake and it come out clean then it's ready.

To make the chocolate sauce, simmer the cocoa powder, cream and the condensed milk together in a pot
and pour it ontop of the cake once it comes out the oven. The sauce is opptional but it makes the cake super moist
