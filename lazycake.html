Hello everyone. Today we have made an easy lazy cake and to make it you'll need:

1 cup of milk
1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
1 teaspoon of cornstarch
2 tablespoons of water
Biscuits of your choice

The direction:

The first step is to:
pour the milk, sugar and cocoa powder into a pot and heat it up.
In a small bowl, mix together the water and cornstarch very well until the lumps are all gone.
make sure that your milk mixture is boiling and pour in the cornstarch mix.After mixing it well, turn off the heat and allow it to rest.

The second step is to crush you Biscuit of your choice into different sized pieces into a large bowl. Pour your hot chocolate sauce into the bowl and mix really well until it becomes sticky not watery.
Place it onto a parchment paper and roll the mixture into a cylinder shape. Wrap it in an aluminum foil, twist the ends and place it in the frezer for 3 to 5 hours or until it gets firm. After patiently waiting, remove it from the foil and chop it into slices.
Arrange the cakes in a plate and pour your chilled chocolate sauce over it. Enjoy it with a cup of coffee!
