Hello everybody! Today we have made a delicious layered desert for any time of the year!
To make this desert youâ€™ll need:

White sauce
1 cup of plain yogurt
2 tablespoons of sweetened condensed milk
3 tablespoons of sugar
3 tablespoons of milk powder

Coffee sauce
1 cup of milk
2 teaspoons of coffee
1 teaspoon of cornstarch
2 tablespoons of sugar
And your choice of biscuits

The Directions

To make the white sauce:
In a large bowl, whisk together the yougurt, sugar and the powdered milk really well
until there are no lumps .
This step is optional, you can pour a little condensed milk if you like depending on how sweet you want it to be.

To make the coffee sauce:
In a pot, add the cup of milk and your coffee of your type, you can adjust the strenght
of the taste to your liking or even add cocoa powder instead and sugar.
Whisk a little and whisk more after adding the cornstarch which helps in thickening your mixture.

Heat it up until it boils and keep on stirring for 2 minutes. Then transfer it to a glass bow. In small cups or large dish,
break some biscuits for the first layer and pour a little of the white sausce, break some biscuits, add it on top
and pour a little of the coffee sauce.
Repeat this process twice to make a beautifull layered dessert.

Dust the top with a little coffee or cocoa powder.
Refregerate the dessert for a couple of hours and serve it with a hot glass of coffee.
